About us

Mission

A well-known educational institution, Little Stars Play School in Nanganallur, Chennai. This pre-school takes pride in the holistic and innovative learning methods which have helped them raise the bar in the field of education. The centre follows a well-researched curriculum that is based on the play way method of learning. This school offers education for children in the age group of 2 to 6 years. There are a host of other supplementary activities that children can enroll in to enhance their skill set. In a populous suburb of Chennai, this centre is situated on the Jain College Staff Colony of Nanganallur. Undoubtedly it is one of the best kindergartens in Nanganallur, Chennai.

Standards Offered

Playgroup, Pre-KG, LKG and UKG
(only 7 - 10 students per standard)

Why Little Stars?

Little Stars Play School in Nanganallur has an environment which is not only child-friendly but engaging for children to learn and have fun at the same time. It has spacious classrooms that are well-appointed with various amenities and facilities. This centre employs caring and involved certified teachers that play an instrumental role in the learning and development of the kids.
